How to make a burger

  1. Take the bun and slice it in half.
  2. Grill the patty until it's cooked to your preference.
  3. Place the patty on the bottom bun.
  4. Add toppings like lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  5. Spread your favorite sauce (ketchup, mayo, mustard).
  6. Place the top bun and serve.
