Lists 1

    Steps

  1. Take all the ingredients
    • Whole wheat flavour
    • Baking powder and baking soda
    • Salt
    • Eggs
    • Butter
    • Unsweetened Applesauce
    • Milk
    • Honey or pure maple syrup
    • Vanilla
  2. Whisk together the dry ingredients: white whole wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients: eggs, applesauce, milk, honey and vanilla.
  4. Whisk the melted butter into the wet ingredients.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix until just combined. The batter may be a little lumpy.
  6. Cook the pancakes on a hot griddle or skillet. You’ll know the pancakes are ready to flip to cook on the second side when bubbles start to form and pop on the top and the bottom side is lightly golden brown.
